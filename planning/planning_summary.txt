PLANNING SUMMARY
Before implementation, the News Portal system was analysed in terms of:
- Functional requirements
- Non-functional requirements
- UI/UX structure
- Database design and normalisation

The system was designed around three main roles:
- Reader
- Journalist
- Editor

The UI was planned to provide:
- A clear landing page
- Registration and login pages
- Role-based dashboards
- Article review and approval for editors
- Public viewing of approved articles

The database was designed with the following models:
- User
- Publisher
- Article
- Newsletter

Relationships were normalised using ForeignKey and ManyToManyField to reduce duplication and improve maintainability.
